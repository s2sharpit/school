## Subject

## Key Directives

Schools affiliated with CBSE are required to develop and maintain a functional website containing comprehensive information, including teacher details and qualifications, as part of **Mandatory Public Disclosure** (Appendix-IX format).

### Relevant Affiliation Bye-law Clauses

**Clause 2.3.8 – WEBSITE**
Schools seeking affiliation shall develop and maintain a website providing all vital information about the school.

**Clause 2.4.9 – WEBSITE**
Every affiliated school will develop a website containing comprehensive information such as:
- Affiliation status
- Infrastructure details
- Teacher details including qualifications
- Number of students
- Postal address, email, telephone numbers
- Copies of transfer certificates issued
- Fee information

**Clause 14.5**
Schools must prepare an annual report containing comprehensive information (name, address, email, telephone, affiliation status, period of affiliation, infrastructure details, academic calendar, teacher qualifications, training details, academic achievements, sports achievements, PTA activities, SMC decisions, number of students, etc.) and post it on the school's website **before 15th September of every year**.

**Clause 5.4**
The pupil-to-teacher ratio should not exceed **30:1**. Additionally, there must be **1.5 teachers per section**, excluding the principal, physical education teacher, and counsellor.

---

## Compliance Deadline

Schools must update teacher qualification information on their website as part of Mandatory Public Disclosure as per the revised **Appendix IX** format latest by **15th February 2026**.

## Penalty for Non-Compliance

Non-compliance will be treated as a violation of **Clause 12.2.3** and may result in penalties as laid down in **Chapter 12** of the Affiliation Bye-laws of the Board.
